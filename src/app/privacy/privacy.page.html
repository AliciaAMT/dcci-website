DCCI Ministries

Last updated: January 2026

1. WHO WE ARE

DCCI Ministries ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website https://dcciministries.com.

We are a Christian ministry based primarily in the United Kingdom, with collaborators and technical administration in the United States.

CONTACT INFORMATION

  • Privacy, content, or theological inquiries:
    info@dcciministries.com
  • Website, technical, or bug reports:

2. OUR COMMITMENT TO PRIVACY

We respect the privacy of our visitors and supporters. We collect only the data necessary to operate this ministry website, communicate with users, and protect the site from abuse.

We do not sell personal data.

3. WHAT DATA WE COLLECT

Depending on how you interact with the site, we may collect the following:

A. INFORMATION YOU PROVIDE VOLUNTARILY

  • Name and email address (contact forms, newsletter signup)
  • Account information (when user accounts and comments are enabled)
  • Messages you send to us

B. AUTOMATICALLY COLLECTED INFORMATION

  • IP address
  • Browser and device information
  • Cookies and similar technologies
  • Interaction data related to embedded media (such as YouTube videos)

4. COOKIES AND EMBEDDED CONTENT

This website uses cookies for:

  • Basic site functionality and security
  • Spam prevention and rate limiting
  • Embedded third-party content (such as YouTube videos)

Embedded YouTube videos may collect data as if you visited YouTube directly. This is governed by Google's privacy policies.

By using this site, you acknowledge the use of cookies required for functionality and embedded media.

5. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

We use or plan to use the following services:

  • Hosting & infrastructure: Firebase (Google)
  • Embedded media: YouTube (Google)
  • Analytics (planned): Google Analytics / Firebase Analytics
  • Spam protection: reCAPTCHA, honeypots, rate limiting
  • Donations (external):
    • PayPal
    • Patreon
    • Cash App

Donations are processed on third-party platforms and are governed by their respective privacy policies. We do not store payment details on our servers.

6. USER ACCOUNTS AND COMMENTS

User accounts and comments are planned features but are not currently available to the general public.

When comments are enabled:

  • Account data will be limited to what is necessary for participation
  • Comments may be moderated
  • Abuse, harassment, or spam will not be tolerated

A separate Comment Policy will apply at that time.

7. HOW WE USE DATA

We use collected data to:

  • Respond to messages and inquiries
  • Send newsletters (if you opt in)
  • Maintain site security and performance
  • Improve the website and its content
  • Moderate future comments or community features

8. DATA RETENTION

We retain personal data only as long as necessary:

  • Contact emails are kept for correspondence records
  • Newsletter data is retained until you unsubscribe
  • Technical logs are retained for security and troubleshooting

9. YOUR RIGHTS (UK GDPR / EU GDPR)

If you are located in the UK or EU, you have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you (read-only access)

At this time, requests for correction or deletion may be limited depending on technical constraints, but we will respond in good faith to all legitimate inquiries.

TO MAKE A REQUEST, CONTACT:
info@dcciministries.com

10. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

This website is intended for adults.

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

11. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy as the site grows or new features are added. The "Last updated" date will always reflect the most recent revision.
