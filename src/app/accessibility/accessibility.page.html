ACCESSIBILITY STATEMENT

DCCI Ministries

Last updated: January 2026

DCCI Ministries is committed to making this website as accessible and usable as possible for all visitors, including people with disabilities.

We strive to follow recognized accessibility best practices and to improve accessibility as the site grows and new features are added.

ACCESSIBILITY SUPPORT

If you experience difficulty accessing any part of this website, encounter a barrier, or have suggestions for improvement, we welcome your feedback.

Please contact us at:
admin@accessiblewebmedia.com

We will make reasonable efforts to address accessibility concerns in good faith.

LIMITATIONS

While we aim for broad accessibility, some third-party content (such as embedded videos or external platforms) may not fully conform to accessibility standards. These elements are governed by their respective providers.

ONGOING COMMITMENT

Accessibility is an ongoing effort. We view it as part of responsible stewardship and service to others.
